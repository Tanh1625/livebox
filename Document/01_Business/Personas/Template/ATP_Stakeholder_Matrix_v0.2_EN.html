Power/Interest Grid Analysis

Mapping the involvement of individuals and departments within the organization (100+ employees) that directly impact the system's development outcomes.

Stakeholder / Role Power Interest Objectives & Expectations BA Management Strategy
Sponsor / Decider
HR Director 		High High Focuses on the big picture. Requires a Dashboard showcasing pass/fail rates and Cost-Per-Hire. Absolute data security is mandatory (no CV leakage). Manage Closely: Present wireframes and request direct approval. Provide weekly progress reports. Notify immediately regarding any project scope risks.
Key User
Recruiter 		Med High Needs a USER-FRIENDLY system. Dislikes manual data entry. Requires auto-email features for bulk candidate rejection. Needs robust CV search functionalities. Keep Satisfied: Conduct deep-dive operational interviews. Allow them to test Prototypes directly for rapid UX/UI feedback.
End User
Hiring Manager 		High Med Rarely visits the internal Web portal. Prefers receiving CVs via email and clicking an "Approve for Interview" or "Reject" button with a quick comment seamlessly on mobile. Keep Informed: Design an extremely clean, Mobile-Responsive UI for this segment. Proactively gather feedback regarding their candidate evaluation process.
IT / Constraint
IT Admin 		Med Med System must comply with company cybersecurity standards (e.g., SSL certificates, password encryption). Must integrate with Windows Active Directory (AD) for login. Consult: Proactively send Non-functional Requirements (NFR) documents for IT review and sign-off to ensure technical architecture integrity.
External User
Candidates 		Low High Expects a quick, streamlined CV submission page. Whether applying via mobile or desktop, the entire process should take under 3 minutes. Monitor (Design Focus): Ask QA Testers to simulate the Candidate role to critique the smoothness and reliability of the CV application flow.

📝 BA Note: Due to the company's 100+ employee scale, Hiring Managers hold significant influence over whether the system is successfully adopted internally. It is crucial to design an Actionable Email feature (allowing rapid CV approval directly from their inbox) to satisfy this busy user group.
